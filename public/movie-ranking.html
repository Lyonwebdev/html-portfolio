My Favorite Movies of All Time

The Top Three

The Return of the King

Amazing action. Outstanding score. Rode of the Rohirrim. Need I say more?

Ready Player One

It's a comfort movie that my wife and I have watched hundreds of times. Anytime it is on, I stop what I am doing and watch it.

The Phantom of the Opera

Another comfort movie with an outstanding soundtrack. Gerard Butler's voice is amazing.
